# Legal Contract Analyser

## Problem Statement

4 out of 5 Indians sign contracts without reading them. 1 in 3 tenants face deposit disputes. People routinely sign bulky legal agreements without understanding the clauses, leading to future financial and emotional stress.

This tool bridges the gap between scanning and reading legal documents — built from two perspectives: **Owners** and **Tenants**.

## Target Users

- Tenants signing rental agreements
- Property owners drafting or reviewing rental agreements

## Features

### 1. Clause Risk Categorisation
Automatically categorises every clause into one of four risk levels:
- **Unfair** — clauses that are one-sided or potentially unlawful
- **High Risk** — clauses with significant financial or legal exposure
- **Medium Risk** — clauses requiring careful consideration
- **Low Risk** — standard, generally acceptable clauses

Analysis is provided from both the **Owner's perspective** and the **Tenant's perspective**.

### 2. AI Chatbot
A conversational chatbot to drill down on key clauses, ask follow-up questions, and get plain-language explanations of complex legal references.

### 3. Hidden Key References & Keywords Detector
Surfaces buried or obscure terms, references to schedules, annexures, or legal statutes that are easy to miss on a quick read.

### 4. Long Document Support
Handles documents of 10 or more pages without loss of context or accuracy.

### 5. Multilingual Support
Supports multiple Indian languages to serve users across regional contexts.

### 6. Get Started Page
An onboarding page listing all available features and guiding new users through the tool.

### 7. Info & Pricing Page
Transparent breakdown of costs for:
- Document analysis
- Chatbot usage

### 8. Tenant vs Owner Bias Score
A quantified bias percentage indicating how heavily a contract favours the owner or the tenant.

### 9. Timeline for Important Dates
Extracts and surfaces critical dates from the contract — notice periods, renewal deadlines, payment due dates, and more.

### 10. Contract Comparison
Side-by-side comparison of two contracts to identify differences in clauses, terms, and risk levels.

## Success Criteria

A user (tenant or owner) can upload a rental agreement and within minutes:
- Understand which clauses are risky and for whom
- Identify hidden or unusual terms
- Get a bias score showing whose interests the contract favours
- Ask follow-up questions via chatbot
- See all important dates on a timeline
- Compare it against another version of the contract
