# Legal Contract Analyser

## Project Overview

A tool that bridges the gap between scanning and reading legal documents. Built for the Indian context where 4 out of 5 people sign contracts without reading them and 1 in 3 tenants face deposit disputes.

Analyses rental agreements from two perspectives: **Owners** and **Tenants**.

## Target Users

- Tenants signing rental agreements
- Property owners drafting or reviewing rental agreements

## Features

### 1. Clause Risk Categorisation
Categorises every clause into one of four risk levels — Unfair, High Risk, Medium Risk, Low Risk — from both the Owner's and Tenant's perspective.

### 2. AI Chatbot
Conversational chatbot for drilling down on key clauses and getting plain-language explanations of complex legal references.

### 3. Hidden Key References & Keywords Detector
Surfaces buried terms, references to schedules, annexures, or legal statutes that are easy to miss.

### 4. Long Document Support
Handles documents of 10 or more pages without loss of context or accuracy.

### 5. Multilingual Support
Supports multiple Indian languages for regional accessibility.

### 6. Get Started Page
Onboarding page listing all features and guiding new users through the tool.

### 7. Info & Pricing Page
Transparent cost breakdown for document analysis and chatbot usage.

### 8. Tenant vs Owner Bias Score
Quantified bias percentage indicating how heavily a contract favours the owner or the tenant.

### 9. Timeline for Important Dates
Extracts critical dates — notice periods, renewal deadlines, payment due dates — from the contract.

### 10. Contract Comparison
Side-by-side comparison of two contracts to identify differences in clauses, terms, and risk levels.

## Success Criteria

A user (tenant or owner) uploads a rental agreement and within minutes can:
- Understand which clauses are risky and for whom
- Identify hidden or unusual terms
- Get a bias score showing whose interests the contract favours
- Ask follow-up questions via chatbot
- See all important dates on a timeline
- Compare it against another version of the contract
